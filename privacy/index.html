Privacy

Your reading is yours alone.

Sojourn is built to be a quiet, private place. We do not collect or store your personal data. What you ask, read, and explore stays on your device.

¶

What we collect

Nothing about you. We do not require an account, and we do not gather your name, contacts, location, or the content of your conversations. Your questions and the passages you open are not sent to us or kept on our servers.

¶

What stays on your device

Your trail, your topics, and your reading history live on your device, under your control. You can clear them at any time. Removing the app removes them with it.

¶

Changes

If this policy ever changes, we will post the update here. Questions are always welcome at hello@trysojourn.app.
